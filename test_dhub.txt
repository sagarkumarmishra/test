### Root Cause (Very Common Issue)

The **"Parameters cell"** (the one you toggled with `# Toggle this cell as "Parameters cell"`) is meant for **interactive runs** in Synapse Studio (when you run the notebook directly).

When the notebook runs **from a Pipeline**, Synapse **ignores** the toggled Parameters cell in many environments and does **not** automatically override the variable.

This is why your token remains empty, the code raises the exception, and the Spark session gets **killed**.

### ✅ Correct Fix (Use the Reliable Pipeline Method)

**Step 1: Remove the "Parameters" toggle**

- Go to your first cell (the one with `bearer_token = ""`).
- Click the **...** (ellipses) → **Toggle parameter cell** again to **untoggle** it.
- The word **"Parameters"** should disappear from the cell.
